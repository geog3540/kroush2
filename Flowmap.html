Flow map

More southern parts of Mexico appeared to have the most significant negative migration flow patterns. Northern Mexico had more people coming into than out of those areas. A lot of the top migration flows are within that southern central part of Mexico where there are a couple areas that receive positive net flows of immigration from the other areas. The volume of migration is more predominant throughout the central part of the country. However, there is some volume of migration that is moving up north. There are a couple places in the central southern part of the country where most people are heading to. Direction of flow is majority toward the center of the southern parts of the country, and northward.
